GPM IMERG Rainfall — GEE Export
================================
Run: uv run python scripts/03_gee_exports.py

Requires: earthengine authenticate (run once)

Exports to Google Drive then download to data/raw/rainfall/

Layers:
  1. Mean annual precipitation 2001-2023
  2. 95th percentile daily precipitation (extreme events)
  3. Annual precipitation 2018-2024 (flood inventory period)
